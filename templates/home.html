About Us

Technology-driven emergency healthcare solution built for faster ambulance coordination and life-saving response.

ResQGo is a smart ambulance management platform designed to reduce emergency response delays using intelligent GPS-based dispatch systems.

The platform integrates real-time ambulance tracking, shortest-route optimization, live monitoring, and centralized emergency coordination.

Built using Flask, Socket.IO, OpenStreetMap APIs, and Geolocation services, ResQGo delivers a modern healthcare emergency experience similar to ride-booking apps.

Our mission is to save lives through fast, reliable, and technology-powered ambulance services.
